Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about VoyageAI and make your travel planning experience even smoother.

What is VoyageAI?

VoyageAI is an AI-powered travel planner that helps users generate personalized itineraries, estimate budgets, discover attractions, and manage trips efficiently.

Is VoyageAI free to use?

Yes. The basic features are completely free. Premium features may be introduced in future updates.

Can I save multiple trips?

Yes. After logging in, you can save and manage multiple trips from the My Trips page.

How is the budget calculated?

The budget estimate considers your destination, duration, travelers, and travel preferences to generate an approximate trip cost.

Does VoyageAI provide weather updates?

Yes. Weather information is displayed for your selected destination using live weather APIs.

Can I edit my itinerary later?

Absolutely. Saved trips can be updated anytime from your dashboard.
